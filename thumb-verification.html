Frequently Asked Questions

Who can use the thumb verification system?

All registered university faculty, staff, and students can use this system. Visitors should use the manual entry system.

What if my thumb isn't recognized?

If your thumb isn't recognized after 3 attempts, please use the manual entry system or contact the university administration office to update your biometric records.

How is my entry/exit data used?

Your entry/exit data is used for campus security and attendance purposes. A daily report is sent to the Dean's office for record-keeping.
