About Bundelkhand University

Bundelkhand University, Jhansi was established in 1975 by the Government of Uttar Pradesh. The University is situated on a sprawling campus of 167 acres of land on Kanpur Road, about 8 km from Jhansi Railway Station.

Our Vision

To emerge as a premier institution of higher learning, recognized nationally and internationally for excellence in teaching, research, and innovation, committed to the holistic development of students and the socio-economic progress of the Bundelkhand region.

Our Mission

  • To provide quality education that fosters intellectual growth and professional competence
  • To promote research and innovation addressing regional and global challenges
  • To nurture ethical values, leadership qualities, and social responsibility among students
  • To contribute to the sustainable development of the Bundelkhand region
